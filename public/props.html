Skyrim Steel Dagger

A re-creation of the steel dagger from Skyrim. This was created by first 3D printing 6 pieces of the dagger. I then glued them all together and sanded them to remove the layer lines. I filled the faults of the print and primed the print. I then added the mettalic paint, weathered in the details, and wrapped the handle.
